CONTRAPTION

Problem Statement/Task:

1. Jerry’s hungry, so he took some of Tom’s cheese. Tom has to design a perfect mousetrap for jerry. Create a crazy contraption and help Tom finally catch jerry. You need to design a complex, clever mechanism.


Event rules:

The participants are required to create a Contraption to complete the following tasks:
A. Minor Tasks: (At least three )
1- Breaking of a Test Tube.
2- Wetting a piece of cloth.
3- Extinguishing a burning candle.
4- Burning of a piece of paper (at least 5cm*5cm) completely.
5- Use of Lever mechanism.
6- Starting of the Contraption using the vibration of Mobile Phone.
B. Major Tasks: (At least three)
1- Bursting of a balloon.
2- Lighting a candle
3- Move a toy car to a distance of at least 60cm, without the use of an inclined plane
4- Filling of a tumbler with at least 100 ml of water.
5- Winding of a wire/thread on a cylinder .The length of wire winded should be at least 25 cm.
6- Cutting a piece of paper (of at least 5 cm width) in two separate parts.
In addition to the above mentioned compulsory tasks, the participants can choose (at their will and convenience) a maximum of one task from the Bonus tasks-

C. Bonus Tasks: (At most one)
1. Projectile throwing of a ball (for at least 30 cm horizontal distance),
and landing it within 15cm*15cm area where jerry is standing (can be a container or a square drawn on paper).
2. Stopping a ball as a part of the contraption (for at least two seconds), and then continuing its motion without an intervention.
3. Shoot a dart to hit the target.
D. Super Task:
Catch jerry in the final step using any mechanisms of choice.


General Rules :

1. A minimum of 2 and maximum of 4 members are allowed in a team.
2. Total time allocated is 3 hrs.
3. The Contraption should last for at least 20 seconds and at most for 300 seconds, excluding the time taken for interventions (if any).
4. A maximum of one human intervention is allowed. Explanation - The initial touch for starting of the contraption is not counted as an intervention.
5. The contraption should have at least 8 steps and at most 25 steps.
6. The contraption should be confined to an area of 2.5*2.5 sq. mtr.
7. The participants are allowed to bring their own materials (for e.g. thermocol, cardboard, motor, table fan, beakers, scissors etc.) for building up the Contraption, subject to reasonable restrictions by the organizers.
8. In case of any discrepancy the decision of the organizers and the judges will be final and binding.
1. 50 points will be given for each Major task completed.
2. 30 points will be given for each Minor task completed.
3. 25 points will be given for each Bonus Task completed.
4. 50 points will be given for the Super Task.
4. 10 Points will be given for each step involving energy conversion (for e.g. Mechanical to Electrical or vice-versa)
5. 10 Points will be given for use of Mechanical and Electrical energy and 15 points for use of Chemical energy.
6. Complexity and Aesthetics will also carry points. (To be awarded from a maximum of 30 points).
7. 20 points will be deducted for first human intervention after the first (which is allowed). Subsequently, 30 points will be deducted for each additional intervention.
8. 50 Points will be given if no human intervention is used (not even the one which is allowed).
9. For every repetitious task, 10 points will be deducted.
10. 10 Points will be given for judicious use of energy.
