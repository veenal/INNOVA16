PANTHEON CUBE

Problem Statement/Task:

Spartacus escaped!!! The news has been that Spartacus now with his army is ready to take on the Romans through the passage of Tyrrhenian sea.’Ceaser’ this time got a change to get involved. Ceaser now have to build an impenetrable boundary made of concrete that can rest on water and can withstand the wrath of Spartacus.
TASK:
 Make a concrete block of dimensions 15x15x15.
 The grade of the concrete should be greater than or equal to M20.
 The concrete block should float on water.
 Required materials will be provided to each team of 2 members.


Event rules:

ROUNDS:
1. Prelims, which will be a theoretical round and will check your credibility for the practical round.
2. Finals, in which you will make the required cube.

TESTING:
1.Compression test will be done to check the compressive strength of the cube.( Test will be done on UTM)
2.Density will test will be done.
