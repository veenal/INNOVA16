ROBO RACE

Problem Statement/Task:

Paul Walker has arrived for race and the opponent has planted the traps in between the track so that you have to make a robot that will survive there traps and destroy the opponent traps so that Paul can pass the track easily.


Event rules:

Objective:-
You have to make a bot mechanism that can destroy the traps like polyethene sheet wall, wooden stick etc. and cover the whole path i.e. full of traps in least time.
Track Specification:-
1. The track width vary from 45cm to 80cm.
2. The obstacles in the path like nails, stones, sand, oil, water, wood pieces, egg trays, mud etc.
3. The path can be inclined, declined, curved etc.
Robot Specification:-
1. The bot size can’t exceeds 35cm because the minimum width of path is 45cm.
2. The bot can resist water.
3. Bot should not contains any flammable material.
4. Bot can be wired or wireless it doesn’t matter on results and we will provide you 220 V AC supply, you can use it if you want.
5. Wired robots, don’t worry, track will be such that least resistance will be offered to the wires of the bots.
Event Rules:-
1. A team may consist of maximum 4 members.
2. The team can be made from different college or schools.
3. The bot width can’t exceed 35cm otherwise it will be disqualified.
4. The event is divided in 3 rounds.
5. For each round there will be different tracks.
6. For skipping of each obstacle 2 min will be added to your total time and for each foul 30 sec to be added.
7. If your bot is out of track then it will be considered as foul.
8. In the race you can’t touch the bot until it completes the track, for each touch we will count it as foul.
9. Don’t destroy the traps until the specified on the trap.
Criterion of Victory:-
At the end of each round the judges will decide the qualifier and winner on the basis of less time taken to travel the path.

General Rules :


1. The judges have rights to disqualify any of the participant for misbehaviour and breaking of rules.
2. Judges decision will be treated as final result and there are no arguing on the result.
3. If any of the rule is changed then we will notify on the site.
