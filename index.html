WELCOME

INNOVA’16 is the 23rd edition of annual technomanagement fest of DTU to be held from 19-21 Feb 2016. INNOVA'16 organised successful guest lecture series and workshops and will see a participation of 15000 people in over 20 technical and non-technical events.

ABOUT

INNOVA, the annual techno-management fest of Delhi Technological University (Formerly Delhi College of Engineering) was founded in 1994, under the Department of Mechanical and Production Engineering and Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering as a celebration of talent and creativity. Over the past 22 years, INNOVA has been a symposium of young minds from numerous colleges across the nation to participate in innovative events and inculcate new technicalities.

Expanding its spectrum and including events of nearly each branch of engineering in its 23rd phase, INNOVA ’16 has adopted the theme “THINK. CREATE. CONQUER.” We aim to provide a platform to young budding engineers to think creatively, showcase their talent and display their skills in unexplored domains. INNOVA being an unequalled and incomparable extravaganza is a perfect dais where ideas and people mingle with each other. INNOVA’16 is anticipated to be a rejuvinating experience for students and associates alike.

EVENTS

DAY 1

SYDNEY COATHANGER

This is a dream-come-true event for a structural engineer. Before bridges are actually constructed, their models are to be made from paper and glue. The strength and aesthetic appeal of these models adjudge the design of the actual structure.

JUNKYARD WARS

The all-time favourite of the technical/creative minds of our junk-ineers. This time junk-ineers are provided with a virtual budget in the beginning, which is followed by a bidding event for different junks they will use in their model.

CLAY PLAY

Show your skills and use clay to make an aerodynamic car to get through all the stages.

CAD CRAFT

It’s all about creating designs using computerised techniques on a given problem statement. This event aims at testing the imagination and spontaneity of the participants using mind-boggling questions and their equally innovative results.

DREAM CITY

Any structure should be able to hold itself in disastrous conditions. The objective is to examine their techniques of building prototypes which resist disastrous conditions.

PANTHEON CUBE

you'll make the best cube out of cement and be tested on your stability



DAY 2

CONTRAPTION

It is based on the genesis of energy; it entails the understanding of complex machinery and the ways they can meditate energy via the primate resource.

BOOMERANG

The event where you have to show your aerodynamic skills and make a boomerang using wood.


STRUCTURE-D

The Structural Design event where the students are given an opportunity of onsite architectural designing. Participants have to tackle every problem which an architect would face.

CONSULTING KNIGHTS

A magnificient platform for budding entrepreneurs to present their B-plans corresponding to a given theme. The participating teams need to pass through a series of hurdles in order to establish a company.

ELECTRIFIED

Use your brain and solve various electronics and logical puzzles.

BID MASTERS

Show your bidding skills and how much you know cricket.

MATRIX REINFORCEMENT

Build columns to support your matrix and build the strongest base.



DAY 3

AIR GLIDER

The biggest news-maker of Innova ‘15 is back where the students are set on a time-bound challenge to make hand-launched gliders. These crafts are then launched from a high-rise and the teams score on the basis of glide time and range.

ROBORACE

The event where you show the speed of your robots. Got a robot? Get on and win the race.

ROBOMAZE

Its not just about speed, get through the hurdles and get out of the maze.

ROBOWARS

It is the evnt where you get your robots in the ring and show whose robot is the best.
