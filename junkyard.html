JUNKYARD WARS

Problem Statement/Task:

The wild folks are planning an attack on the Castle Black. The men at nights watch are out of artillery. So they decided to harm the wild folks with their self-made weapon, made out of the available stuff there.
Task: Save Castle Black Jon Snow!!!


Event rules:

Round 1
Elimination round:- A written Aptitude test will be conducted to shortlist the teams for round 2. Maximum of 2 team members can give test together.
Round 2
The range test:-The teams will have to make a weapon that hits the maximum range with a given ammo (balls). The points will be awarded according to the range of the weapon. The time allotted to build the weapon is 2 hrs
Round 3
Destruction test:-The teams will have to hit the target or have to cause maximum destruction (in case of multiple target).The points will be awarded according to the number of targets hit. The teams will be given 15 min. to modify their weapon according to round 3.

Don’t forget to bring your teammates and your brain…….
 The material provided will consist of metal and wooden bars, sheets, cans and other discarded out of use materials. For joining purposes tape, hammer, iron nails etc. may be provided.
 Maximum team size: 5
 Construction equipment and materials will be given on site.
 The judging criterion will be disclosed at the time of event.

General Rules :

1. The teams participating should create a healthy game spirit.
2. Misbehaviour of any kind will lead to disqualification of the team by the judges.
3. Judges decision will be treated final and no arguing shall be allowed.
4. The judges can changes the above stated rules. Any change in the rules will be displayed in the website or will be stated at the time of event.
