CLAY PLAY

Problem Statement/Task:

The teams are required to sketch their dream car and create a clay model of it with clay as per the rules mentioned. Car model should be made under aerodynamic consideration.

Model Specifications:
Minimum height of the model should be 70mm.


Event rules:

Rounds:
Round 1: Prelims
This is a written test based on aptitude and technical knowledge of participants in the field of car modelling and its aerodynamic features.
Round 2: Wind Tunnel Testing
Clay models will be tested in the wind tunnel at the venue.
Round 3: Incline Test
All the models will be allowed to move down the incline and cover the maximum horizontal distance possible.
Scoring Criteria:
Total Score= Score in round 1+ round2 + round3 Team Size: Maximum 4 members are allowed.

General Rules :

1. All the required material to make the clay model will be provided in round 2 to qualifying participants which includes clay, foam brick, chassis and other necessary things.
2. Judges decision will be final and binding in case of any discrepancies.
