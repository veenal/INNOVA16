TORPEDO

Problem Statement/Task:

It’s Rocket Science.
Take your first big step to propel yourself into the world of aerodynamics!
Remember Newton Baba’s third law of motion?
Problem Statement/Task:
Design any stage water rocket (Torpedo) using a plastic container preferably a soft drink bottle that uses a combination of pressurized air and water for propulsion to complete a defined task.


Event rules:

Arena details: This will be updated soon.
Design specifications:
1. Soft drink bottles should be used preferably.
2. No part of the rocket (fins, nose cone etc.) must detach during the flight. Points will be deducted for the same.
3. One team can have at max. two torpedoes.
4. Participants must bring their own launcher. Every part of the rocket must be made in the arena only.
Team Size: Maximum 3 members.
Rounds:
Round 1: Strategy making (written exam)
Round 2: Fire it Complete the task in battlefield.
Scoring Criteria will be disclosed in the battlefield.
General Rules:
1. The judges have rights to disqualify any of the participant for misbehaviour and breaking of rules.
2. Judges decision will be treated as final result and there are no arguing on the result.
3. If any of the rule is change then we will notify on the site.
