SYDNEY COATHANGER

Problem Statement/Task:

Nicknamed " The Coathnager" for its arche design. The Worlds sixth longest steel arch's span is 530m and was completed in 1916. 50 years later in 1966, the public was allowed to walk across the deck of the bridge and it will be repeated in 2016. The whole Australia is excited for the centenary of the bridge to observe the megastructure keenly.The bridge is expected to be overcrowded and this may cause some change in the structural behaviour of the bridge. So lets create our own bridge to observe the mechanisms.
TASK:
Create an Economical bridge for a given span length and maximum load carrying capacity using the given material.
SPECIFICATIONS:
minimum span length of deck = 25cm
hieght of the pieces = 20cm
