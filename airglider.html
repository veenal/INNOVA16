AIR GLIDER

Problem Statement/Task:

Design a hand-launched air glider to be judged on the basis of gliding time, gliding distance and design report.


Event rules:

1. A maximum of 4 participants are allowed. Lone wolf are welcomed.
2. All necessary material will be provided including balsa wood, sand paper, pencil, cutter, clay etc.
3. Participants are allowed to bring their sketches or any material explaining their design. (Not necessary, they can make sketches on the spot.)
4. A member of the team have to launch the glider from a specific launch point.

Glider specifications:
Minimum wingspan should be 24”. Minimum length of fuselage should be 12”.
Scoring criteria:
Round 1: Prelims (30 points) This is a written test. (Topic: General aptitude and basics of aerodynamics)
Round 2: Fly High This is the fabricating round allowing participants to make their air glider.
1. Points will be given to the air glider on the basis of its time of flight, gliding distance and design report or explanation.
2. A team having maximum time of flight will be awarded 30 points. Team having time next to highest will be given 25 points and so on (20, 15, 10….). Same will be followed for gliding distance.
3. Only the displacement of the glider will be considered as the gliding distance and not any curved path if followed by the glider. A standard measuring tape will be used to measure the distance.
4. Design report/Explanation: (Max. 10 points)
5. Team scoring maximum points overall will win.


General Rules :

1. The judges have rights to disqualify any of the participant for misbehaviour and breaking of rules.
2. Judges decision will be treated as final result and there are no arguing on the result.
3. If any of the rule is change then we will notify on the site.
