DREAM CITY

Problem Statement/Task:

Human beings have discovered a new method to solidify clouds. The most amazing thing that happened along is," The beta rays entering through Ozone holes at poles , has made the air denser. And so the human beings have evolved along ,with this new earth. ". Miraculously, the solidified clouds float in the air. The 196 countries from all over the world , have joined their hands to celebrate this discovery. A new flying land has taken birth. The new democratic government consists of all the people from all around the world. The main headquarter being situated in the clouds over " The Land of Dreams". The people at main land, know this " Land of Dreams " as "India". And So, they call this new flying land over India as," THE DREAM CITY. "
TASK:
Design a well planned city on a cardboard which should be least bothered by disasters. Challenge is that the designed city should be economical and aesthetic as well. Required material will be provided.


Event rules:

ROUNDS:
1. Prelims, which will be a theoretical round and will check your credibility for the practical round.
2. Finals, in which you will design and prepare a model of your DREAM CITY.

TESTING:
1. Vibration test.
2. Wind and Impact testing
3. Aesthetics will be checked.
