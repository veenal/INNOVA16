CADCRAFT

Problem Statement/Task:

Doing Da Vinci!!! Da Vinci owns a factory of wrist watches in California. Recently, his very talented CAD designer abdicated his job. His production of watches has come to a standstill and at the same time he has to fulfill the order of 5000 wrist watches. Now, he is in dead need of experienced CAD designer. As wrist watches are deployed of epicyclic gear train, he conducts a recruitment test for the same.


Event rules:

1. Participants have to design an epicyclic gear train which provides a gear ratio of 1:9
2. Further, participants need to enclose that train in a gear box.


General Rules :

Participants must bring their own system to work on.
• Participants are free to use any CAD designing software like Solidworks, CATIA, PRO-E etc.
• It’s not a team event.
• Maximum Time limit is 3 hour
• All the students enrolled in high school, undergraduate, postgraduate (excluding PhD.) program at any recognized institute (identity card will be checked) are eligible to participate.
• Participants are allowed to use design library feature of the software.
• Rules are liable to minor changes which will be updated on the site.

Scoring Criteria
1. Basic sketch of design (10 marks)
2. Calculation : calculate no. of teeth of sun & planet gears for 1:9 gear ratio (20 marks)
3. Design of Subcomponents required with specifications. (100 marks)
4. ASSEMBLY of all subcomponents (100 marks)
5. Simulating animation of gear train mechanism (50 marks )
6. Extra 10 points will be awarded to the participant whoever finishes the task in minimum time.
