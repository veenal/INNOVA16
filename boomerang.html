BOOMERANG

Problem Statement/Task:

Mowgli has lost his boomerang somewhere in the Seeonee jungle. Sher Khan is closing in fast. This cruel, cunning, rebellious and menacing tiger will not leave him unharmed. Neither Bageera nor Baloo is around. Only you can save him now. Make a returning boomerang so that Mowgli can save himself.
Task: Defy Gravity! Design and fabricate a returning boomerang which when thrown returns to the thrower at its starting point.


Event rules:

Scoring:
Each thrower has three attempts.
Landing in the centre circle is worth ten points; the next circle out is worth 8 points, and so through to 6, 4, and 2 points. If you land on a line, you get the average of the two surrounding circles â€” so stopping on the edge of the centre circle is worth 9 points.
The total score will be the sum of the points obtained in three attempts.
There will be two rounds:

Round 1: Prelims
A written paper testing general aptitude and technical knowledge of the basic physics behind boomerang flight.
Round 2: Final Showdown
Participants clearing round 1 will be allowed to perform the given task of fabricating a returning boomerang.
1. The boomerang must be thrown from the centre circle.
2. A throw must reach out to the 20m radius circle to be legal.

General Rules :

1. A team may consist of maximum 3 members.
2. The team can be made from different college or schools.
3. The judges have rights to disqualify any of the participant for misbehavior and breaking of rules.
4. Judges decision will be treated as final result and there are no arguing on the result.
5. If any of the rule is change then we will notify on the site.
6. Material will be provided on the spot necessary for making a returning boomerang which will include hard card board, filer, sand paper and other things necessary.
7. Participants can choose any type of boomerang to make with any no. of arms they choose, only condition is that it should be a returning one.

What do you require to bring?
Your teammates and your brain!!!!!!!!
